# Getting Started · Resident

A one-page orientation for registering your vehicles, inviting guests, and managing your parking — all in one place. Deep-link into the reference docs for full detail.

## Getting set up

1. **Register your account.** Scan your property's QR code or use the link from your property manager, then verify your email. See [Resident Self-Registration](/help/resident-self-registration).
2. **Add your vehicle.** Enter plate, make, model, and color. Your property manager approves it — you'll see the status change to approved in your portal.
3. **Check your status.** Each vehicle shows pending, approved, or declined right in your portal. If something's declined, you'll see the reason.
4. **Invite a guest.** Issue a visitor pass so your guest can park without being towed. Passes auto-expire — nothing to cancel. See [Visitor Passes](/help/visitor-passes).
5. **Request a space.** If your property offers reserved spaces, request one. Your manager assigns it and you'll see it in your portal. See [Spaces and Reserved Parking](/help/spaces-and-reserved-parking).

## What you'll do most

- Add or update your vehicles (color, make, model) · Issue visitor passes for guests · Check approval status (pending / approved / declined) · Request or view your reserved space · Mark updates as read when something's approved

## Good to know

- **You're notified in-portal.** When a manager approves or declines something, it shows in your portal with a "mark as read" — no email to dig through.
- **Some details are locked.** You can edit your vehicle's color, make, and model. Your plate and unit are set by your property — ask your manager to change those.
- **Guests park on passes.** A visitor pass is how a guest parks without being towed. Issue one before they arrive.
