# Getting Started · Property Manager

A one-page orientation for the person running the day-to-day at a property. Covers what to do first, what you'll do most, and the tools that matter. Deep-link into the reference docs for full detail.

## Start here

1. **Sign in.** Your portal opens to the properties assigned to you. Everything you do is scoped to those properties — you won't see anyone else's. See [Signup and First Login](/help/signup-and-first-login).
2. **Check your queue.** Pending items need your call: resident registrations, vehicle approvals, and space requests. Approve or decline — the resident sees the outcome in their own portal. See [Resident Management](/help/resident-management).
3. **Look up a plate.** Your fastest tool — type any plate to see status: green (registered resident), gold (active visitor pass), amber (under review), or red (not authorized). Read-only — it never tows. See [Property Management Overview](/help/property-management-overview).
4. **Manage spaces.** Assign and track reserved spaces. When a resident requests one, it lands in your queue — you approve by assigning. See [Spaces and Reserved Parking](/help/spaces-and-reserved-parking).
5. **Issue visitor passes.** Create a pass for a guest, or let residents issue their own. Passes auto-expire, so there's nothing to cancel. See [Visitor Passes](/help/visitor-passes).

## What you'll do most

- Approve or decline residents & vehicles · Assign and manage reserved spaces · Issue and review visitor passes · Look up plates · Respond to disputes · Watch Insights for activity at your properties

## Good to know

- **Property-scoped.** You only see and manage the properties assigned to you — nothing from other properties or companies.
- **Lookups are read-only.** Plate Lookup shows authorization status. It never issues a citation or tow — the next step is always your decision.
- **Everything's tracked.** Approvals, passes, and space assignments are all logged, so there's always a record.
