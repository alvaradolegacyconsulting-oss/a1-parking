# Getting Started · Company Admin

A one-page orientation for the account owner. Covers the five things to do on day one, plus the operating rhythm after that. If you want the full detail on any step, follow the link — the flyer's steps deep-link into the reference docs.

## The five day-one steps

1. **Sign in and set your password.** Your welcome email has your temp login. Confirm your email, change your password, and you're in.
2. **Complete your account profile.** Company name, contact info, tow-service license (Texas requires this), and your logo. See [Account Setup](/help/account-setup).
3. **Add your first property.** Every property gets its own reserved-space pool, its own residents, its own visitor-pass rules. See [Adding Properties](/help/adding-properties).
4. **Assign a Property Manager.** Give them the property so residents and vehicles get approved without you as the bottleneck.
5. **Confirm your billing plan.** Track (PM-Only / Enforcement-Only / Legacy) and per-property + per-permit counts drive your bill. See [Billing and Tier Changes](/help/billing-and-tier-changes).

## What you'll do most

- Add and manage properties · Assign PMs and leasing agents · Approve escalations · Track platform-wide activity · Handle billing and account settings

## Good to know

- **You see everything company-wide.** Your PMs see only their assigned properties. Residents see only their own accounts.
- **Approval authority.** By default, you approve residents and vehicles. You can grant that authority to Property Managers via [Approval Authority Grants](/help/approval-authority-grants) — the PM sees an "Approve" button; you keep the ability to override.
- **Read-only lookups.** Plate Lookup and Property Manager tools are read-only — they never issue a citation or tow.
