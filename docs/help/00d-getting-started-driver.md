# Getting Started · Driver

A one-page orientation for the tow / enforcement operator in the field. Covers the daily workflow — plate lookup, evidence capture, tow ticket generation. Deep-link into the reference docs for full detail.

## Start here

1. **Sign in.** Your driver portal opens to your assigned company. Every plate lookup, photo, and tow ticket is stamped with your credentials. See [Signup and First Login](/help/signup-and-first-login).
2. **Look up a plate.** Type any plate to see status: green (authorized), gold (visitor pass), amber (under review), or red (not authorized). Read-only — the tool never tows. Your decision, always.
3. **Capture evidence.** For any violation, take clear photos (front + rear plate, vehicle in context). See [Submitting Violations](/help/submitting-violations).
4. **Generate a tow ticket.** Once evidence is captured, generate the tow ticket right from the field. It stamps time, location, plate, driver, and the reason code. See [Tow Tickets and Evidence](/help/tow-tickets-and-evidence).
5. **Confirm the tow.** After the vehicle is towed, mark the ticket as completed. Your company admin and the residents see the full record.

## What you'll do most

- Plate lookups · Photo and video evidence capture · Tow ticket generation and reprint · Marking tickets complete · Reviewing your own recent activity

## Good to know

- **Read-only lookups.** Plate Lookup never issues a citation or tow. The decision is yours; the tool documents it.
- **Everything's stamped.** Every action is tagged with your credentials + timestamp + GPS. That's your protection in a dispute.
- **Evidence is required.** No photos = no tow ticket. Take the time up-front — it saves the dispute later.
