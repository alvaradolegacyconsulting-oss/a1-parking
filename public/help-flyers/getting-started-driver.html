Scan handles formatting

Type plates however you have them written — capitals, spaces, and dashes are handled automatically.

Tow tickets show your company's logo and details — set by your company admin, so every ticket looks official.

Evidence stays attached

Photos and video are stored with the violation, so the record is always complete.
